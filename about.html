Our Story

Guru Nanak Hospital is a multi-specialty tertiary care center located in Bidar, Karnataka, known for medical excellence and affordable care. Established in 1969 by Shri Panth Ratan Shiromani Sardar Joga Singh ji, it began as a modest 25-bed facility and has grown to 100 beds, serving patients from all walks of life. Guided by Dr. Balbir Singh and Dr. Reshma Kaur, the hospital is known for compassionate, patient-centric care.

Throughout our history, we have remained committed to our core values of compassion, integrity, and innovation, ensuring that every patient receives the best possible care and treatment.

Our Community Impact

At Gurunanak Hospital, we believe in giving back to the community that has supported us for over five decades. Our commitment extends beyond the walls of our hospital, reaching out to make a positive impact on the health and well-being of our entire community.

  • Free health camps and screenings in underserved areas
  • Educational programs on health and wellness in schools
  • Support for local health initiatives and charities
  • Environmental sustainability programs within our facilities
