Frequently Asked Questions

What are your visiting hours?

Our general visiting hours are from 10:00 AM to 8:00 PM daily. However, these may vary for different departments. Please check with the specific ward or call our reception for more details.

How can I schedule an appointment?

You can schedule an appointment by calling our appointment desk at +91 1234567890, through our website's online booking system, or by visiting our outpatient department in person.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we accept a wide range of insurance providers. Please contact our billing department or check our website for a list of accepted insurance plans. We recommend verifying your coverage before your visit.

What should I bring for my first appointment?

For your first appointment, please bring a valid ID, your insurance card (if applicable), a list of current medications, and any relevant medical records or test results. Arriving 15 minutes early to complete necessary paperwork is recommended.

Is parking available at the hospital?

Yes, we provide ample parking space for our patients and visitors. There is a dedicated parking area near the main entrance, and valet parking services are also available for your convenience.
